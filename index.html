Frequently Asked Questions

What is Compliance as a Service?

+

Compliance as a Service is a solution designed to simplify regulatory compliance for businesses, automating most tasks to make compliance realtime and not an audit activity.

Is this service suitable for small businesses?

+

Absolutely! Compliance as a Service is scalable and works for businesses of all sizes, offering cost-effective solutions.

What is AI as a Service?

+

AI as a Service simplifies AI adoption for businesses by managing infrastructure setup, data processing, model training, and deployment. We enable organizations to leverage AI without requiring extensive technical expertise or high capital expenditure.

Do I need a technical team to use AI as a Service?

+

Not at all. Our team handles the technical complexities, so you can focus on your business goals.

What does Privacy as a Service include?

+

Privacy as a Service empowers businesses and individuals to manage their data and privacy effectively. Features include privacy awareness tracking, identification of services holding user data, and the creation of a personalized privacy heatmap.
